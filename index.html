Book of DUMB Illustrations

Hi, So my story is, I was good at copying somewhat like Hatake Kakashi. And there used to be these Anime cards and stickers when I was growing up. I'd Sketch those Images in the Sticker and Show off, yeah like "look what I have here!!!". Boys would go crazy like,"AAAAA bro please give me this drawing". Another one would be like What! man you are good at this, you're super cool. Well, the girl's reactions were not that great, they'd say "wow you are very talented" and all. But any amount of attention I got was superb the while it lasted. This is when I was about 10. After few years: I started giving less mind to what others think hence my book of dumb Illustrations has whatever like I really dont care.

But now I strive to find joy in the things I do. I plan to make things that hold meaning and joy hopefully I've still got it. I welcome you to partake in my joyous journey.
